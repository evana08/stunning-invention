Fun Facts About Dogs

Dogs have an incredible sense of smell, with some breeds being able to detect certain diseases. Additionally:

  • A dog's nose print is unique, much like a human fingerprint.
  • Dogs dream, and they experience rapid eye movement (REM) sleep, similar to humans.
  • The Basenji dog breed is known as the "barkless dog" because it doesn't bark like other dogs.
